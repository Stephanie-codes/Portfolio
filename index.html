About Me

My name is Stephanie Burlinchon, and I am a self-taught JavaScript developer. I come from a background in Mathematics, where I honed my analytical and problem-solving skills. Graduating from Queen Mary University of London in 2011.

After spending 10 years in administration, from Personal Assistant to Office Manager, I decided to pursue a career change and dive into the world of technology. I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have since been developing my skills as a developer.

What I love about coding is the endless potential for problem-solving and the satisfaction that comes with creating something from scratch. I enjoy working on challenging projects and finding creative solutions to complex problems.
